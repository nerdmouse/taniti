Restaurants

There are many locations for dining during your stay especially if you want to try fresh, local options, and many are located in Merriton Landing! We have five locations that serve local, freshly caught fish and locally grown rice for an authentic Taniti cuisine! If you're wanting a taste closer to home, we also have 3 American-style and two Pan-Asian cuisine restaurants.

Many may not want to go out to eat, and they may want to try cooking at their lodging instead. No worries, we have you covered as well! We have two supermarkets to find fresh local goods as well as imported options! If you're just looking local, there's two smaller grocery stores to check out as well!

If you're looking for more adult options, our island has its own microbrewery you can visit for a taste of local favorites! Taniti City also has several pubs if you're wanting food and drink. Just stay mindful of alchohol serving times located on the about page.

If you have any interest in a reservation at one of our local restaurants, please check out the partnered reservation site, RealDiningReservations.
