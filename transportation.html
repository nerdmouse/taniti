Transportation

We understand that when choosing to stay at Taniti, you'll need to find out the best ways to not only get around the island, but how you'll also be able to arrive here to begin your stay with us!

Flights

The most important part of getting here would be booking a flight to the island! Due to the size of our island, we can currently accommodate small jets and propeller planes to at our modest airport. However, we are in the process of expanding our airport to allow larger jets to land on the island within the next few years.

If you would like to check on booking a flight, please follow this link.

Cruise Options

Another major way to reach our island would be to take a cruise ship through our partnered cruise line, TotallyRealCruises. They would bring you directly to Yellow Leaf Bay where they would stay for one night before departing for a week. If you're wanting more information, please go to their website here!

Local Transportation

Local transportation is handled through a few different ways. We have our public busses that serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. If you're looking for other options, private buses serve the rest of the island. Taxis can also be rented here for additional transportation around Taniti City as well as the option to rent a car from our local rental agency, TotallyRealRentals. You can reserve a vehicle in advance through their site. If neither of those options interest you, Taniti City is highly walkable especially around Merriton Landing where many of our hotels are located. If you want to know more about lodging options, please visit the Tourism section.
