Tourism

Needing a Hotel?

We have plenty of options to choose when you're looking for lodging. There are many locally owned hotels, and a number of bed and breakfasts. If you're looking for something more extravagent, please make sure to check out our one and only four star hotel, TotallyRealHotel, at their site! Otherwise, check out our local options through this site instead!

Check out some of the sights while here!

There's plenty of tourist attractions to experience while visiting Taniti. Whether its hiking in the rainforest, snorkling and playing at the beach, or visiting our museum and art gallery, there should be activities for everyone!

Local Culture

Are you someone who appreciates learning about the culture and history? Make sure to visit our local history museum which showcases how we became the wonderful island we are today! If you prefer art, you can also visit our art gallery. Both are located in Merriton Landing, and you can find more information for both of these here!

Exploring Taniti City

Looking for activities near TotallyRealHotel? Not to worry! We have local movie theater to catch the newest films while you're on vacation, or there's always the arcade.

Wanting something more physical? Make sure to check out our bowling alley as well. There will also be a nine-hole golf course operational next year which will add to the fun in Taniti City! Of course, you also can't forget Yellow Leaf Bay which boasts beautiful white, sandy beaches.

There are also plenty of places to eat and drink nearby which you can find out more on the restaurants page.

Finding Nature and Beauty

Wanting to find options outside of the city? You won't be disappointed! Take a ride on a helicopter to view the island from new heights, or book a chartered fishing tour to check out the ocean! While out towards the ocean, you can also take a snorkeling trip. More information can be found by checking out our partner, LegitWaterCo, where you can book in advance!

Trying to find opportunities away from the shore? Check out the local zipline course through the rainforest! While making your way there, you can also check out the hiking trails spread throughout the forest! You can also make the trek up close to our volcano. You can find out more through LegitRainforestCo for information about these opportunities as well as maps!
